3D Modelling

The Magic Crystal the VR project

This is a VR project for the first trimester in 2018.

The crystal in the center contains huge energy. And this crystal is protected by the hand sculptural around it. This center weathered through the years, the wall broken, andmoss come out on the ground and the center, but the strong crystal is still running and giving energy.

Gaming Project

An Introductory Course in Truancy

For this project we designed a escape game- An introductory course in truancy. The idea is the student who is day dreaming in the media lab. In his dream he found he was trapped in the room and has to escape. It is a unfinished project. It is game demo and only has low ploy model at the stage. I worked with a partner who is studying computer science.He does the coding work andI focus on the modelling and the demo video..

Industry design

The Polyhedron Drum

I made this drum by modelling and 3D printing. This project is to make a drum. I am inspired by the polyhedron audio equipment. This drum can give difference voice once you knock on the different side and faces. The industry is a really interesting subject and I had a lot of fun from the modelling and 3D printing.
